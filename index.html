Home

The Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) program at PESCE, launched in partnership with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) in the academic year 2023-2024 with an intake of 60 students, aims to bridge the gap between technology and business. This interdisciplinary program prepares students for the multifaceted challenges of the digital age by integrating business principles with computer science education.
